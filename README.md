# FUTURE_CS_02

## Phishing Email Analysis

### Objective
The objective of this project is to identify phishing characteristics in a suspicious email and raise awareness about common phishing techniques.

### Indicators of Phishing
- Fake sender email: `support@paypaI-security.com` (uses a capital **I** instead of a lowercase **l**).
- Urgent subject line: **"Urgent! Verify Your Account"**.
- Generic greeting: **"Dear Customer"**.
- Suspicious link: `http://paypal-login-security.com` (not the official PayPal website).
- Threatens account closure within 24 hours.
- Creates panic and pressure to make the user click the malicious link.

### Prevention Tips
- Verify the sender's email address.
- Avoid clicking suspicious links.
- Enable Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA).
- Never share passwords or personal information through email.
- Report phishing emails to the email provider or organization.

### Conclusion
This email is a phishing attempt because it uses urgency, a fake sender address, a suspicious URL, and threats to trick users into revealing their account information.
